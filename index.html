CELIA OKECHUKWU An actress and Commercial Model

PROFILE

Actress & Commercial Model

Priscillia Okechukwu who hails from Imo State, is a 27 year old graduate of mass communication from the prestigious University of Nigeria, Nsukka. She has always been interested in acting and ventured into it after graduating from the University. She has been in productions such as; Shattered Crown TV Series, Oasis TV series, Simi's Aimasiko video, The first born(an opera), permolit paint advert, Sterling bank women empowerment short film, Oga! Pastor, Banana Republic web series, Rholi & Zehra web series, Muno sings music video, Cadbury ad; to mention but a few. She is diversified, so has played roles ranging from neglected wife, sister, fan, police officer, reporter and so on. When she isn't trying to take over the acting world, she writes songs, sings, is an avid reader, and hosts karaoke, loves to dance even though it's a work in progress. Her dream is to one day be a force to reckon with, leaving an impact through her acting and music.
